Vegetable Curry

Easy Prep 20 mins 2 £4.17

  • 1 tablespoon oil to fry
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic chopped finely
  • 1 eating apple cut into medium chunks
  • 1 potato cubed
  • 400g can chick peas
  • 1 mug of water
  • 2 tablespoons of curry paste
  • veg stock cube crumbled
  • 1 courgette sliced
  • Quater mug of sultanas
  • 4 mushrooms sliced
  • 1 tablespoon tomato purée
  • 2 tablespoons of yougurt, crème fraîche or soured cream
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh mint or 2 tablespoons of dried mint
  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan and fry onions until soft.
  2. Add the garlic, apple, potatoes and chickpeas. Cook in the oil for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the water, curry paste and stock cube then bring to the boil and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the courgette, sultanas, mushrooms and tomato purée. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. To make the rice, bring water to boil. Add rice and salt and cook for 18 minutes without removing the lid.
  6. Add the mint to the yogurt to make the Raita.

Spicy Vegetable Pasta Bake

Easy Prep 15 mins Cook 25 mins 2-3 £4.78

  • 1 and a half mugs of pasta
  • 1 tablespoon oil to fry
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic finely chopped
  • 6 mushrooms sliced
  • 2 courgettes sliced
  • Half red or green pepper sliced
  • 1 teaspoon mixed herbs
  • 1 red chilli chopped finely
  • 1 tin condensed cream of tomato soup
  • Half mug of grated cheese
  1. Preheat oven 200°C fan / Gas mark 7
  2. Cook the pasta by boiling water in a pan. Once the water is boiling, gently lower the pasta into the water. Cook for 6-12 minutes depending on type of pasta.
  3. Heat a little oil in a frying pan and fry the onions and garlic salt until soft.
  4. Add the mushrooms, courgettes, peppers, herbs and chilli. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add the tomato soup and bring to a boil and take off the heat. Season well.
  6. Drain the pasta and stir into vegetable mixture.
  7. Turn into a greased casserole dish and top with grated cheese.
  8. Cook for 25 minutes or until cheese is browned.

Loaded Baked Potato

Easy Prep 10 Cook 1hr 15 mins 2 £4.56

  • 2 baking potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 red chilli
  • 2 tablespoons of mayo
  • 1 tin of tuna
  • 2 spring onions finely chopped
  • 1 tin of sweetcorn
  • 100g chedder cheese grated
  • Quater lemon for the juice
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C fan / Gas mark 6. Rub the potatoes with the oil and season with salt, then transfer to a baking tray. Bake for 1 hr 15 mins or until the skin is crispy and the flesh inside is tender. Alternatively, microwave the potatoes for 5 mins on high, then rub with oil and season, before baking for 30-35 mins to crisp up.
  2. Meanwhile, mix the mayonnaise, then mix with the tuna, spring onions, sweetcorn, cheese, chilli. Stir in a squeeze of lemon.
  3. Cut open the cooked potatoes and season; top with the tuna mix. Serve with the fresh spring onion.
